# Glory Be (English)
----
**Glory be to the Father,**  
and to the Son,  
and to the Holy Spirit,  
as it was in the beginning,  
is now, and ever shall be,  
world without end.  
**Amen.**

# Gloria Patri (Latin)
----
**Glória Pátri,**  
et Fílio,  
et Spirítui Sáncto.  
Sícut érat in princípio,  
et nunc, et sémper,  
et in sáecula saeculórum.  
**Amen.**
